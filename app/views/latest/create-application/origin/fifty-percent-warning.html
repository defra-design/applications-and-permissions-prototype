You might receive 50% less compensation if the cattle moved into a TB breakdown herd and are removed as a reactors or direct contacts before they gain OTF (officially Tuberculosis free) status.

This does not apply if:

  • the destination herd is registered to a bovine TB health scheme accredited before the breakdown began under the Cattle Health Certification Standards (CHECS)
  • the cattle are being moved to Approved Finishing Units (AFU), Approved Finishing Units (Enhanced) with Grazing (AFUE), Licensed Finishing Units (LFU) and TB Isolation Units (TB IUs)
