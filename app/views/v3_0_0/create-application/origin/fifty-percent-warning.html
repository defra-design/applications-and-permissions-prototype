For animals which have moved into a TB breakdown herd that are then removed as TB reactors or direct contacts before the herd regains officially TB free (OTF) status, a 50% reduction in compensation is applied to these animals.

This does not apply if:

  • the destination herd is registered to a bovine Tb health scheme. Accreditation from the Cattle Health Certification Standards (CHECS) must be in place before the breakdown began
  • the cattle are moving to any type of approved or licenced finishing unit, or to a TB isolation unit

For more information, read the slaughter and compensation section on the GOV.UK Bovine TB guidance page (opens in new tab).
