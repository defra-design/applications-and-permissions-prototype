If cattle are removed as reactors from a TB breakdown herd before they gain OTF (officially Tuberculosis free) status, you might receive 50% less compensation.

This does not apply if:

  • the destination herd is registered to a bovine TB health scheme (before the breakdown began)
  • the cattle are moving to any type of approved or licenced finishing unit, or to a TB isolation unit

For more information, read the slaughter and compensation section on the GOV.UK Bovine TB guidance page.
