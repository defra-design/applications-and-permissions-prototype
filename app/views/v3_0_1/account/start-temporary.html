Get permission to move animals under disease controls

What this service can be used for

You can use this service to apply for a Bovine Tuberculosis (TB) movement licence.

Before animals move onto or off a farm or premises with TB restrictions, you must apply for a licence with the Animal and Plant Health Agency (APHA).

Moving animals off a TB restricted farm to go to slaughter

If you are moving animals from a TB restricted farm to slaughter, you do not need to use this service to apply for a licence.

You should have a general licence in the information pack sent to you from APHA. The pack was posted to you when your herd was placed under TB restriction.

This licence allows you to move animals:

  • from a TB restricted farm direct to slaughter
  • via a slaughter gathering/ red market (if the licence was issued after 30 September 2024)

Moving animals onto a TB restricted premises

The service does not currently support moving animals from a TB restricted premises to a TB restricted premises. To apply for a licence for this movement type complete this GOV.UK application form.

If your farm or premises is not under TB restrictions and animals are moving onto a TB restricted farm, it is the responsibility of the TB restricted farm to apply for a licence. We need details about their biosecurity practices to process the licence.

For more information before you apply for a licence, see the Bovine TB: get your cattle tested in England guidance.

Before you start

Licences cannot be assessed or issued if:

  • reactors are still on your farm or premises
  • cattle are awaiting skin or blood test results
  • TB testing is overdue

Contact your vet if you are unsure whether the tests have been sent. If APHA has not received the tests, there could be delays in processing your licence.

To apply for a licence, you need the following information:

  • name of the County Parish Holding (CPH) owner
  • County Parish Holding (CPH) number and address where the animals are moving from
  • where the animals are moving to, for example the type of market
  • email address to send the licence to

It can take up to 5 working days to process your application. It may take longer if veterinary checks are required, or we need further information.

Get help

If you need help using the service you can call 03000 200 301 and choose the option for TB licencing. Lines are open from Monday to Friday between 8.30am and 5pm.
