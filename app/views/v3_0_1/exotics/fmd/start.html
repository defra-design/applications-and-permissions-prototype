Move animals under disease controls

Use this service to apply for a movement licence during a foot and mouth disease outbreak.

What you can apply for

You can use this service to apply for a licence to:

  • move live animals to slaughter on or off your farm or premises
  • move carcasses
  • move milk products

Before you start

APHA need these details to process your application:

  • details about the origin, including who is responsible for the farm or premises
  • ID numbers if you are moving animals
  • reason and dates for the movement

Using and sharing your information

How we use your personal data is set out in the exotic, notifiable and reportable diseases privacy notice (opens in a new tab) .
