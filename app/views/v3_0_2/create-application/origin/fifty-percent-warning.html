Animals that enter a TB breakdown herd and are later removed as TB reactors or direct contacts before the herd is officially TB-free will receive a 50% reduction in compensation.

This does not apply if:

  • the destination herd is registered to a Bovine TB health scheme. Accreditation from the Cattle Health Certification Standards (CHECS) must be in place before the breakdown began
  • the animals are moving to any type of approved or licenced finishing unit, or to a TB isolation unit

For more information, read the slaughter and compensation section on the GOV.UK Bovine TB guidance page (opens in new tab).
